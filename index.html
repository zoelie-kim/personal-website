EEG Gait Decoder

ML & Neurotechnology

Built a hybrid TCN-LSTM pipeline to decode gait phase and ankle angle from 64-channel EEG data, targeting real-time BCI control for neuroprosthetics and robotic exoskeletons. The TCN detects heel-strikes and outputs a latent vector; the LSTM uses this alongside cyclically encoded gait phase to predict continuous ankle angle. Achieved a mean AUC of 0.924 for heel-strike detection and R² of 0.735 for ankle angle prediction. Presented at conference with Neurotech@Berkeley, April 2026.
